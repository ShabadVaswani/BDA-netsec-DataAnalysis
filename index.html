09

LSTM Stress Forecasting

Predicting future physiological states

  • Objective: Predict next-minute Stress Level & Heart Rate.
  • Architecture: Stacked LSTM (64/32 units) with Dropout (0.2).
  • Input: Sliding window of 60 minutes history.
  • Key Insight: Stress is autoregressive; past stress predicts future stress better than external factors.
