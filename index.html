RouteSense Screen Detection

Behavioral inference from network patterns

Method

Infer screen activity from network handshake patterns. Binary Heuristic: screen_on = 1 if upload_bytes > 1MB/min

Insights

Captures stress patterns, attention switching, sleep disruption, and cognitive load. OS-agnostic and privacy-preserving.

09

LSTM Stress Forecasting

Predicting future physiological states

Objective

Predict next-minute Stress Level & Heart Rate.

Architecture

Stacked LSTM (64/32 units) with Dropout (0.2). Input: Sliding window of 60 minutes.

Key Insight

Stress is autoregressive; past stress predicts future stress better than external factors.
